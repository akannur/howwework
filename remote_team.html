How our remote team works?

For now, we have remote teams in India and Costarica. There is a daily standup meeting held in Denver Dish office which is been taken care by the Product Owner, all the scrum masters have to participate whether it is the direct communication or can be GoToMeeting. Later on Scrum Masters communicates with his/her to team in helping dev team to pick stories based priority. Everyday a work status will be sent to the Scrum Master by the dev team members which includes blockers if any, on what they worked today and what they will be working on tomorrow.
